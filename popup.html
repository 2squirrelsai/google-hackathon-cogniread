👋 Welcome to CogniRead!
CogniRead transforms how you read on the web by adapting content to your unique needs. Whether you have dyslexia, ADHD, or simply want to read more efficiently, CogniRead provides AI-powered tools to make every webpage more accessible.
🚀 Getting Started
1
Visit any webpage with text content (articles, blogs, documentation)
2
Click the CogniRead icon and press "Activate on this Page"
3
Look for the floating control panel (⭐ icon) on the page
4
Enable features that help you read better using the toggles
5
Use keyboard shortcuts for quick access to Focus and TL;DR modes
✨ Available Features
💡 Simplification Levels
Adjust text complexity with 5 levels: Off, ELI5, ELI10, ELI15, and College. Explains complex concepts in age-appropriate language for better comprehension.
🔠 Text Expansion
Expands abbreviations and acronyms automatically. Helps readers understand technical documents without constantly looking up terms.
🔄 Sentence Restructuring
Breaks down complex sentences into simpler structures. Improves readability by reducing cognitive load and clarifying meaning.
▶️ Active Voice
Converts passive voice to active voice for clearer, more direct communication. Makes text more engaging and easier to follow.
🎵 Tone Adjustment
Change content tone to Casual, Formal, Encouraging, or Neutral. Adapts writing style to match your reading preferences and context.
🔆 Focus Mode Ctrl+Shift+F
Shows one section at a time to eliminate distractions. Navigate with arrow keys. Ideal for readers with ADHD or attention difficulties.
📝 TL;DR Mode Ctrl+Shift+T
Generates bullet-point summaries of long paragraphs using Chrome's AI. Save time and get the key points quickly without reading everything.
📄 Distraction-Free Mode Ctrl+Shift+D
Transforms pages into clean reader view by removing ads, sidebars, and navigation. Safari Reader Mode for any website with customizable fonts and themes.
📖 Dyslexia-Friendly
Applies OpenDyslexic font, increased letter spacing, and improved line height. Reduces visual stress for dyslexic readers.
📚 Word Definitions
Hover over difficult words to see instant definitions and pronunciations. Expand your vocabulary while reading naturally.
💬 Literal Language
Detects and explains idioms, metaphors, and figures of speech. Essential for neurodivergent readers and non-native speakers.
🔗 Concept Connections AI
Identifies key concepts and shows connections between ideas. Builds knowledge graphs to help you understand how topics relate to each other.
🌐 Plain Language Translation AI
Converts legal, medical, or academic jargon into plain English. Choose specific domain or use auto-detect to simplify complex terminology.
📋 Prerequisites Check AI
Analyzes articles to identify prerequisite knowledge needed. Shows what concepts you should understand first before diving into the content.
🎯 Reading Goals AI
Determines learning objectives, key topics, difficulty level, and estimated reading time. Helps you decide if content matches your goals.
📊 Difficulty Heatmap AI
Visualizes cognitive load with color-coded difficulty ratings. Highlights complex sections so you can pace yourself through challenging content.

💡 Pro Tips
Activate CogniRead only when you need it - it won't auto-inject on pages
Combine multiple features for a personalized reading experience that suits your needs
Use Focus Mode with TL;DR to quickly scan long articles one section at a time
Try Simplification Levels from ELI5 to College to find your optimal reading complexity
Enable Literal Language if you're learning English or are neurodivergent
Use Prerequisites Check before starting technical articles to see what you need to know
Plain Language Translation works great for legal, medical, and academic documents
Difficulty Heatmap shows you where to slow down in complex articles
The control panel can be minimized - just click the ⭐ icon or drag it to a new position
Right-click on any page and select "Activate CogniRead" for quick access
Right-click selected text for "Alternative Phrasings" or "Explain with Analogy"
All AI features run locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in AI - no data is sent to external servers
